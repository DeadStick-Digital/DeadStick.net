Fastest path: send a note through the DeadStick Digital message form. Include your device, operating system version, app version, and what you were trying to do. Do not send private files, photos, contacts, full file paths, passwords, tokens, provisioning profiles, or payment details.

What DeadStick Utilities Can And Cannot Do

DeadStick Utilities can help review platform-allowed cleanup and organization surfaces. On iPhone and iPad, that means Photos-style review flows, Contacts backup and field review, user-selected Files or storage coaching where implemented, privacy review, settings, and StoreKit purchase or restore controls.

DeadStick Utilities does not provide iPhone/iPad protected system cleanup, hidden app cache cleanup, RAM cleanup, malware cleanup, or private app-container cleanup.

Before You Contact Support

  • Keep your own backups before changing files, contacts, or photo libraries.
  • If the app provides a support export, review it before sharing anything.
  • For Contacts, export or keep a local backup before any future merge path.
  • For Photos, review queued items before any future apply path.

Pro Subscription Help
